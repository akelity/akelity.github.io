A very short time ago, in a nearby galaxy (practically here and now), the force that pervades the universe
encountered and connected composable architectures with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Their synergy
promises to revolutionize the development of Gen-AI applications, transforming months of work into just hours.
In this talk, we will explore how modern ML/AI technologies, applied to real-world scenarios, can benefit from a
composable approach to quickly address complex needs with contextualized and updated data. We will discuss the
challenges related to the implementation of these scalable solutions and the crucial role of Platform Engineering
in reducing complexity and accelerating innovation. We will explore how the power of advanced architectures and
platform engineering strategies can radically transform the landscape of “intelligent application” development.
