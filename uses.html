Tools and Gadgets I use: software, devices and more

Explore the software I use, the gadgets I love and other things I regularly rely on for my work and hobbies. Get a glimpse into the tools that help me stay productive and creative.

Workstation

  • Mac Mini, M2 Pro, 16GB RAM (2024)

    For my hobbies, I've been using a Mac Mini M2 Pro (16GB of RAM) for the past few months. This is the first time in the last 15 years that I've had a fixed workstation for my hobby projects, and I am extremely satisfied. The speed and responsiveness are exactly what I was looking for.

  • Apple MacBook Air, M1, 8GB RAM (2020)

    I use the Apple MacBook Air M1 (2020) with 8GB of RAM for on-the-go productivity. Its lightweight design and powerful performance make it an ideal companion for work and travel.

  • LG 34WP75CP Monitor 34" QHD UltraWide Curved LED

    The LG 34WP75CP 34" QHD UltraWide Curved LED monitor provides a stunning and immersive viewing experience, perfect for multitasking and productivity.

  • Bose Noise Cancelling Headphones 700

    The Bose Noise Cancelling Headphones 700 provide exceptional sound quality and noise cancellation, making them perfect for both work and leisure. With a comfortable design and advanced features, these headphones enhance my focus and listening experience.

  • Elgato Stream Deck+

    The Elgato Stream Deck+ enhances my productivity and creativity by providing customizable control over my workflow and streaming setup.

  • Elgato Wave Mic Arm (Low Profile)

    The Elgato Wave Mic Arm (Low Profile) offers a sleek and flexible solution for positioning my microphone, ensuring optimal audio quality during recordings.

  • Rode NT-USB Microphone

    The Rode NT-USB microphone delivers crystal-clear sound quality for my recordings and streaming sessions, making it an essential part of my audio setup.

  • Elgato Key Light (2x)

    The Elgato Key Lights provide professional lighting for my workspace, ensuring excellent video quality for my streams and video recordings.

  • Canon M50 Mark II

    The Canon m50 mark II is my go-to camera for high-quality video content, offering superb image quality and versatile features.

  • Elgato Cam Link 4K

    The Elgato Cam Link 4K allows me to use my Canon m50 mark II as a high-quality webcam, enhancing the visual quality of my video calls and streams.

  • Ikea Uppspel Standing Desk (1.80m)

    The Uppspel standing desk (1.80m) provides a flexible and ergonomic workspace, allowing me to switch between sitting and standing positions for better comfort and productivity.

  • SecretLab TITAN Evo Chair

    The SecretLab TITAN Evo chair offers exceptional comfort and support during long working sessions, contributing to a more ergonomic workspace.

  • Ikea Uppspel Pegboard 2x

    The UPPSPEL pegboards help me keep my workspace organized and clutter-free, providing convenient storage for my tools and accessories.
