We will dive into the dynamic field of platform engineering, a driving force in reducing cognitive load and increasing developer productivity. Through a series of fascinating case studies, we will explore concrete examples that demonstrate the significant impact of these practices on daily operations. We will place particular emphasis on new tools and technologies, which are at the forefront of this revolution. The talk will also include innovative methods for evaluating the effectiveness of platform engineering, providing concrete strategies to enhance productivity in the workflow. Join us to discover the potential of platform engineering in reshaping the software development landscape.
