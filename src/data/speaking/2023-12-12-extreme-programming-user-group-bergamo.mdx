In the ever-evolving world of software development, new practices and tools constantly emerge to improve the quality and efficiency of development. In this talk, we will explore the fascinating world of Platform Engineering and Internal Developer Portals, two fundamental components of the modern ecosystem.

We will define the key concepts of these methodologies and tools, and examine how they can revolutionize the software development process. The main goal is to provide a comprehensive overview of these topics, dispelling any doubts about their relevance within the DevOps philosophy and their ability to positively transform the Developer Experience.
